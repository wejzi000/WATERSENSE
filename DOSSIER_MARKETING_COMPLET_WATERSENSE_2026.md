### Unit Economics (Par Client)

**Pricing Tiers:**
- Essential (3 sensors): 49€/month = 588€/year (10% customers)
- Standard (8 sensors): 129€/month = 1,548€/year (45% customers)
- Premium (25+ sensors): 299€/month = 3,588€/year (45% customers)

**ARPU (Average Revenue Per User): 2,224€/year**

**Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
- Cost per lead: 35€
- Conversion rate: 8% (lead → customer)
- **CAC = 35€ / 0.08 = 437€**
- **CAC Payback period: 3 months** (2,224€ / 12 months = 185€/month > 437€ quickly)

**Lifetime Value (LTV):**
- Gross margin: 77%
- Churn rate: 7%/year (after Y1)
- **LTV = (2,224€ × 0.77) / 0.07 = 24,542€**
- **LTV/CAC ratio: 24,542 / 437 = 56.2** ✅ (EXCELLENT - target >3)

**Retention & Churn Analysis:**
- Y1 retention: 95% (5% churn - early, but acceptable)
- Y2+ retention: 93% (7% churn - mature level)
- Churn drivers: ROI concerns (14%), sensor failures (8%), farm changes (6%)
- Mitigation: Onboarding, 3-year warranty, data migration tools

- Impact: HIGH (30-40% revenue reduction)
- Probability: MEDIUM (agriculture = traditional)
- Mitigation:
  - Free 2-week trials (no credit card)

  - Case studies + farmer testimonials

- Impact: MEDIUM
